Our process

The process of development layed out in plain words
Below is a typical workflow you will see by working with us.

Get in touch

Let us hear the challenge. We have established 3 communication channels for ease of contacting us - send us an email directly at hello@wearea2.com, call our office at +37124118199, or via a form we have setup for your convinience. We will process your request and get back to you as soon as we can.

Let us get to know the project

Let us sign the non disclosure agreement and get to know your project. Our engineers will put their utmost thought in recognizing the challenge. We will go through your requirements and have everything ready for the next steps.

Confirm our proposed solution

This is the most intensive part of the job, grasping the challenge, identifying the ways to solve the puzzle. Let our engineers work on your problem. We will propose a course of actions to tackle down the challenge. We want to cover all bases and prepare for future growth of the product.

Development

We follow Agile Development practices to deliver the product meeting customer's expectations. Never keeping our clients in the dark, we report the progress and issues we encounter. Our transparent communcation setting us apart from the competition.

Release the product and training

Customers expect top of the line product with no corners cut. Our goal is to provide such a product. Each release is tested by our specialists but expecting customers to test the features of the product before final sign-off by the customer. We offer online training for the software we have built not just leaving you with manuals to hang on to.
